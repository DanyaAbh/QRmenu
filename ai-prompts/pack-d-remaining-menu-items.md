Generate grouped isolated food and drink image sheets for a mobile QR menu website for a family creperie, bakery, and cafe named Yanina.

These are not ads, not hero banners, and not lifestyle photos. They are production image sheets for later manual cropping into separate menu-card assets.

Overall style for every image:

- realistic premium food and cafe drink packshot
- square 1:1 image
- transparent background or perfectly cut-out isolated objects
- soft warm natural studio lighting
- clean premium cafe styling
- subtle natural shadow is allowed
- no text, no logo, no hands, no people
- no packaging unless the item itself requires a cup, glass, bowl, plate, or pizza board
- no decorative props
- no lifestyle scene
- no cartoon look
- no plastic 3D icon look
- all objects must feel like one consistent menu asset series
- each object must be clearly separated and easy to crop later

Layout rules:

- Food images: place up to 6 food items per image, arranged in a clean 2x3 grid with generous empty space between items.
- Drink images: place up to 9 drinks per image, arranged in a clean 3x3 grid with generous empty space between items.
- If the final sheet has fewer items than the layout capacity, keep the same grid spacing and leave the extra spaces empty.
- Do not add duplicate items just to fill the grid.
- Do not include captions, numbers, labels, or any text inside the images.

Do not generate images for the section "Вода и соки". Do not include water bottles, packaged juices, cola cans, Lipton bottles, or any factory-packaged drinks.
